# Master Services Agreement — Grady LLC

**Effective Date:** November 15, 2023
**Parties:** Acme Corp ("Customer") and Grady LLC ("Vendor")

---

## 1. Services

Vendor shall provide enterprise communication and collaboration platform services, including email gateway, document management, and API integrations as described in the applicable Order Form and SOW.

---

## 2. Liability

**[LOW RISK]** Each party's aggregate liability for any claims arising under or related to this Agreement is capped at the total fees paid by Customer in the six (6) months preceding the claim. Both parties mutually exclude indirect, consequential, and punitive damages. The cap does not apply to breaches of confidentiality, infringement of intellectual property rights, or fraud.

---

## 3. Data Processing and Privacy

**[CLEAN]** A fully executed and current Data Processing Addendum ("DPA") is incorporated into this Agreement as Exhibit A. The DPA covers GDPR Article 28 compliance, updated 2021 SCCs, CCPA service provider obligations, a quarterly-updated sub-processor list, and Customer's right to request data deletion within 30 days of termination. Annual compliance reviews are scheduled.

---

## 4. Payment Terms

**[CLEAN]** All invoices are due Net 30 days from the invoice date. Customer may dispute any invoice line item within 20 days without penalty. Undisputed amounts remain due. Annual price adjustments require 90 days' written notice and are capped at CPI + 2%.

---

## 5. Intellectual Property

**[MEDIUM RISK]** All custom integrations, workflows, and configurations developed by Vendor specifically for Customer under any SOW shall be assigned to Vendor upon delivery. Customer receives a perpetual, non-exclusive, non-transferable license to use such deliverables solely in connection with the platform. Customer may not independently modify, sublicense, or port deliverables to third-party systems. Vendor retains rights to reuse and commercialize any custom work with other customers.

---

## 6. Renewal and Termination

**[MEDIUM RISK]** This Agreement automatically renews for two (2) year terms unless either party provides 120 days' written notice of non-renewal before the end of the current term.

**[MEDIUM RISK]** If Customer terminates this Agreement for convenience prior to the end of a term, Customer shall pay an early termination fee equal to 50% of the remaining fees due for the unexpired term. Vendor may terminate for convenience with 90 days' notice without penalty.

---

## 7. Service Level Agreement

Vendor commits to 99.95% monthly uptime. SLA credits are 15% of monthly fees per hour of excess downtime, capped at 25% of the monthly fee. Credits are issued as invoice credits on the following month's invoice. Vendor provides quarterly SLA performance reports.

---

## 8. Security

Vendor holds SOC 2 Type II certification (renewed annually) and ISO 27001. Annual penetration testing reports are shared with Customer upon execution of a mutual NDA. Breach notification is within 24 hours of confirmed discovery. Vendor maintains a documented incident response plan available upon request.

---

## 9. Governing Law

This Agreement is governed by the laws of the Commonwealth of Massachusetts. Disputes shall be resolved in the state or federal courts located in Suffolk County, Massachusetts.
