# Master Services Agreement — Bergstrom Inc

**Effective Date:** June 1, 2023
**Parties:** Acme Corp ("Customer") and Bergstrom Inc ("Vendor")

---

## 1. Services

Vendor shall provide SaaS analytics platform access, onboarding, and standard support as described in the applicable Order Form. All services are delivered remotely via web browser interface.

---

## 2. Liability

**[LOW RISK]** Vendor's total aggregate liability for any and all claims arising under or related to this Agreement shall not exceed the total fees paid by Customer in the twelve (12) months preceding the claim. Both parties mutually exclude liability for indirect, consequential, incidental, or punitive damages. This limitation does not apply to Customer's payment obligations or either party's confidentiality obligations.

---

## 3. Data Processing and Privacy

**[CLEAN]** A current and fully executed Data Processing Addendum is incorporated herein by reference as Exhibit B. The DPA includes updated Standard Contractual Clauses (EU SCCs 2021), CCPA service provider terms, a current sub-processor list (updated quarterly), and data deletion obligations within 30 days of contract termination.

---

## 4. Payment Terms

**[CLEAN]** All invoices are due Net 30 days from the invoice date. Customer may dispute invoices in good faith within 15 days. Vendor may not suspend services during active good-faith disputes. Late payment interest is 0.5% per month. Annual pricing adjustments are capped at CPI or 3%, whichever is lower.

---

## 5. Renewal and Termination

**[CLEAN]** The initial term is one (1) year. Either party may terminate this Agreement for convenience with 90 days' written notice. Auto-renewal requires 60 days' notice to cancel. No early termination penalties apply to termination for convenience after the initial term.

---

## 6. Service Level Agreement

**[MEDIUM RISK]** Vendor commits to 99.9% monthly uptime. SLA credits of 10% per hour of excess downtime are available, capped at 20% of the monthly fee. The Agreement does not require Vendor to maintain or provide results of SOC 2 Type II audits; Vendor references a self-assessed security questionnaire only.

---

## 7. Security

**[MEDIUM RISK]** Vendor does not hold SOC 2 Type II certification. Security posture is documented via an annual self-assessment questionnaire (SAQ). Vendor commits to annual third-party penetration testing and will share high-level summaries upon request. Breach notification is within 72 hours of discovery.

---

## 8. Intellectual Property

All data submitted by Customer remains Customer's exclusive property. Vendor may use anonymized, aggregated data for product improvement purposes. Vendor retains all rights to the platform, including enhancements commissioned by Customer unless separately agreed in writing.

---

## 9. Governing Law

This Agreement is governed by the laws of the State of California. Any disputes shall be resolved by binding arbitration under JAMS rules in San Francisco, California.
