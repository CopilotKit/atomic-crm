# Master Services Agreement — Okuneva Group

**Effective Date:** September 1, 2022
**Parties:** Acme Corp ("Customer") and Okuneva Group Ltd ("Vendor")

---

## 1. Services

Vendor shall provide enterprise data integration and workflow automation services. Vendor reserves the right to modify, deprecate, or replace service features at any time with 14 days' notice.

---

## 2. Liability

**[HIGH RISK — REVENUE-BASED PRICING]** Annual fees are calculated as 8% of Customer's gross revenue attributed to workflows processed through Vendor's platform, with no cap on total fees payable. Vendor may audit Customer's revenue records annually with 5 business days' notice. Disputed audit results default to Vendor's calculation unless Customer provides written rebuttal within 10 days.

---

## 3. Data Processing and Privacy

**[HIGH RISK]** This Agreement contains no data deletion or data return clause. Upon termination, Vendor retains the right to retain Customer data indefinitely for "legal, regulatory, and business continuity purposes." No timeline for data destruction is specified. No DPA has been executed. Vendor has not represented compliance with GDPR, CCPA, or any other data protection regulation. Customer data may be shared with Vendor affiliates without notice.

---

## 4. Payment Terms

**[HIGH RISK]** All invoices are due Net 120 days from the invoice date. However, Vendor may assess a "processing surcharge" of 3% per month on any invoice not paid within 30 days, notwithstanding the Net 120 terms. Customer waives the right to offset or withhold any payments, including amounts subject to good-faith dispute.

---

## 5. Renewal and Termination

**[HIGH RISK]** Vendor grants Customer a perpetual license to use the platform. This Agreement does not include a termination for convenience clause. Customer may only terminate for material uncured breach after a 90-day cure period and a 60-day arbitration cooling-off period. There is no exit right upon change of control or acquisition of Vendor. The license is non-transferable and non-sublicensable.

---

## 6. Service Level Agreement

**[MEDIUM RISK]** Vendor commits to 99.0% monthly uptime (approximately 7.3 hours of allowable downtime per month). Scheduled maintenance windows of up to 12 hours per month are excluded from SLA calculations. Vendor's sole obligation for SLA non-conformance is a credit of $500 per incident, regardless of actual impact. Customer must file SLA claims within 5 days of the incident or forfeit the credit.

---

## 7. Security

Vendor represents that it follows "industry-standard security practices" but provides no certification, audit report, or specific control framework. No penetration testing results are available for Customer review. Breach notification timeline is "as soon as reasonably practicable," with no specific SLA. Vendor is not liable for any damages resulting from a security incident.

---

## 8. Governing Law

This Agreement is governed by the laws of the Cayman Islands. All disputes shall be resolved by arbitration in the Cayman Islands under ICC rules. Customer waives any right to participate in a class or consolidated action.
