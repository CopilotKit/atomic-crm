**[HIGH RISK]** This Agreement does not include a Data Processing Addendum ("DPA"). No data processing terms have been negotiated or executed. ---

## 4. Payment Terms

**[MEDIUM RISK]** All invoices are due Net 90 days from the invoice date. Late payments shall accrue interest at 1.5% per month. Vendor may suspend services upon 5 days' notice for overdue amounts. Customer may not withhold payment for disputed line items without Vendor's written consent.

---

## 5. Renewal and Term

**[MEDIUM RISK]** This Agreement shall automatically renew for successive three (3) year terms unless either party provides written notice of non-renewal at least 180 days prior to the end of the then-current term. Pricing for renewal terms shall increase by no more than 15% per annum over the prior term's rates.

---

## 6. Service Level Agreement

**[LOW RISK]** Vendor commits to 99.5% monthly uptime. In the event of downtime exceeding the SLA, Customer's sole remedy shall be service credits not to exceed 5% of the monthly fee for the affected month. Credits do not apply to downtime caused by Customer actions, third-party failures, or scheduled maintenance windows of up to 8 hours per month.

---

## 7. Security

Vendor maintains SOC 2 Type II certification and undergoes annual third-party penetration testing. Vendor will notify Customer of confirmed data breaches within 72 hours of discovery. Security audit reports are available upon written request subject to NDA.
