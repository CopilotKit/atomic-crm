# Master Services Agreement — Gottlieb & Sons

**Effective Date:** March 15, 2023
**Parties:** Acme Corp ("Customer") and Gottlieb & Sons Inc ("Vendor")

---

## 1. Services

Vendor shall provide cloud hosting and managed infrastructure services as described in applicable Statements of Work. Services include 24/7 monitoring, incident response, and quarterly business reviews.

---

## 2. Liability

**[LOW RISK]** Each party's total aggregate liability arising out of or related to this Agreement shall not exceed two times (2x) the total fees paid or payable by Customer in the twelve (12) months immediately preceding the event giving rise to the claim. Consequential, indirect, and punitive damages are mutually excluded. This cap does not apply to breaches of confidentiality or gross negligence.

---

## 3. Data Processing and Privacy

**[MEDIUM RISK]** A Data Processing Addendum ("DPA") was executed on March 15, 2021 and has not been updated since. The DPA references GDPR Article 28 requirements and standard contractual clauses (SCCs) from the pre-Schrems II era. The DPA does not reflect updated SCCs adopted by the European Commission in June 2021, nor does it address CCPA obligations. Sub-processor list is provided but was last updated in 2022.

---

## 4. Payment Terms

**[LOW RISK]** All invoices are due Net 60 days from the invoice date. Customer may dispute invoice line items in good faith within 30 days, and undisputed amounts remain due. Late fees apply at 1% per month on overdue balances. Vendor may not suspend services for disputed amounts under active good-faith dispute resolution.

---

## 5. Renewal and Term

**[LOW RISK]** This Agreement automatically renews for one (1) year terms unless either party provides 90 days' written notice of non-renewal prior to the end of the then-current term. Renewal pricing is subject to CPI adjustment capped at 5% annually.

**[MEDIUM RISK]** Neither party has the right to terminate this Agreement for convenience during the initial or any renewal term. Termination is permitted only for material uncured breach (30-day cure period) or insolvency.

---

## 6. Service Level Agreement

Vendor commits to 99.9% monthly uptime. In the event of non-conformance, Customer is entitled to service credits of 10% of monthly fees per percentage point below the SLA threshold, up to 30% of the monthly fee. Credits are Customer's sole remedy for uptime failures.

---

## 7. Security

Vendor holds SOC 2 Type II and ISO 27001 certifications. Annual penetration testing results are shared with Customer upon written request. Vendor shall notify Customer of any confirmed security incident within 48 hours of discovery.

---

## 8. Governing Law

This Agreement is governed by the laws of the State of New York. Disputes shall be resolved in the state or federal courts located in New York County.
